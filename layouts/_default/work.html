  • Ridgefield Drive

    Garry and Arlene came to us with very vague instructions. They simply said that they wanted their current landscape to have more wow. In the world of renovations that can mean sooo many things. The truth is that they knew they wanted more but didn’t know what more looked liked. Some would think this is great, a chance to just be creative and “have at it”, but the truth is that clients who don’t know what they want are the hardest to deal with. We presented the Dutchaks with a rendering of what we thought their new landscape should look like, it included a new layout that focused on curb appeal. The Dutchaks lot is a corner lot with high exposure, so it was important that the impressive parts of the garden were out for people to see. In the end Garry said that he never envisioned something so colourful and grand.
