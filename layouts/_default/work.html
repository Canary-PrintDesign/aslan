  • Ridgefield Drive

    Garry and Arlene came to us with very vague instructions. They simply said they wanted their current landscape to have more 'wow'. In the world of landscape renovations, 'wow' can mean sooo many things. The truth is they knew they wanted more but didn’t know what more looked liked. Some would think this is great - a chance to just be creative and “have at it”, but often the clients who don’t know what they want are the hardest to deal with. We presented them with a rendering of what we thought their new landscape should look like, which included a new layout that focused on curb appeal. The lot is a corner lot with high exposure, so it was important that the impressive parts of the garden were out for people to see. When we were finished, Garry said "I never could have envisioned something so colorful and grand!"
